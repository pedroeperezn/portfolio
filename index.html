Everything Must Go!

Everything Must Go! | Unreal Engine | C++ | FMOD

Everything Must Go! Is an online multiplayer racing game in which each player is a contestant in a shopping game show. Their goal is to collect and checkout as many items as they can before the time runs out. Players can also mess with their fellow shoppers by ramming into them and use power-ups such as speed-boosts, infinite cart space, shields, and deploying hazards.

Where is My Mind?

Where is my Mind? | Unity | C# | FMOD

Where is my Mind? is a single player puzzle game in which you play as an insomniac man who wakes up trapped in an insane asylum which only inhabitants are the mute and disturbed patients. The player needs to figure out how to scape the asylum by solving a series of puzzles with nothing more but the help of a kalimba.
