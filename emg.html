Everything Must Go!

Everything Must Go! is an online multiplayer racing game in which players are contestants of a game show in which must collect and checkout as many items as they can before they run out of time. Players can also interfere with their rivals by ramming into them in order to make them spill items and also can grab and trigger power-ups.
